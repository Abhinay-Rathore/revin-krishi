Precision Agriculture

Farm & Crop Profiling

Allows for detail mapping of farm plots and crop profiling to have a unfied data for informed decision-making. Capture essential details of farm & crop via mobile app. Creates unique farm IDs for all subsequent activities. Integrate IoTs to the farms for real-time data collection. Utilise geotagging mark fields, measure acreages, and create field maps for spatial understanding.

Crop Planning & Monitoring

It helps in planning your season in advance using a data-driven approach and leveraging insights to optimise overall crop performance. Activity calendars include nutrition, irrigation, pest, and disease schedules and beneficial agriculture practices. Uses farm, crop, weather, GIS and influential data to help in crafting personalised packages of practices. Remotely monitor farms using remote sensing technology for early problem identification, crop growth indicators, and yield forecasting. Prepare crop plans and assign it to respective farm IDs.

Task Management & Automation

It enables efficient resource allocation and workflow optimisation, contributing to increased productivity and sustainability in agriculture. Assign and monitor tasks for farms, field officers, and supervisors Automatic tasks assignments upon generation of alerts and reminders from GIS and IoTs Mark each activity as complete to measure parameters like nutrition usage, activity cost, carbon emission and more. Integration to inventory and farm financials.

Crop Advisory Management

It facilitates proactive farmer engagement and knowledge sharing, digitally empowering farmers to increase their farm productivity. Send weather notifications, alerts, and personalised advice to farmers via the mobile app. Allow farmers to upload images/videos of problems and request expert advice. Provide prompt recommendations and product purchasing options via m-commerce. Send crop health-based notifications and alerts by analyzing geospatial images.

Agribusiness Management

It ensures efficient resource utilisation and financial transparency, enabling strategic decisions for sustainable growth. Update and track inventory levels of seeds, fertilisers, pesticides, and other farm inputs and associated prices. Monitor stock movement and usage, and set up alerts for low stock levels. Track farm expenses at activity level and manage operating costs. Generate profit and loss statements per farm ID and seamless integration with accounting softwares.

Insightful Data Visualization

Dashboards and analytics empowers agribusinesses with detail analytics to identify bottle-necks and trends. Visualize data through charts, graphs, and maps for easier interpretation and control role specific access. Utilize AI assistance for data extraction and analysis. Real time updates on field activities, crop growth status, resource usage etc Overview of key farm metrics such as crop yields, acreage, estimated harvest etc.
